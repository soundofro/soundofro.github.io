Rohith Abraham: ofRO

Rohith Abraham is a music producer, keyboard programmer, sound engineer, with 10+ years of experience working in multimedia, television, film, app development and advertising. He started playing classical piano at an early age having performed at various stages as a young singer. Upon graduating from Loyola College, Chennai, he gained a deeper understanding of media by majoring in Visual Communication. He went on to graduate from SAE Byron Bay/Melbourne with a degree in Audio Production. He worked on independent records in Australia as a Mix Engineer alongside producing his own music under the moniker: ofRO.

Currently he resides in India, working with film composers like Harris Jayaraj and Imman on blockbuster films in the tamil movie industry such as Yennai Arindhaal, Anegan, Nanben Da, Gethu and Rajnimurugan, to name a few.
